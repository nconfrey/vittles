As a Chicagoan

Get a taste of Chicago without ever leaving campus.

Do you feel like you'd like to explore the amazing Chicago food, but just don't have the time to travel all the way downtown? Do you want to try new food but just don't know where to go? Vittles will be a hosting a gourmet food event featuring a prominant restaurant from the Chicagoland area.

We go to the restaurant so you don't have to. Vittles procures tasting menu food sizes from specific restaurants and delivers them to campus for the special event. Interested? Sign up for our listhost and you'll be the first to know the details of our events.

As a Restaurant Owner

We want to bring you more mouths.

Right now, you can only advertise through positive Yelp reviews and word of mouth. But it doesn't have to be this way. By partnering with Vittles, you ensure that your food gets into the mouths of potential foodies in a way that has never been possible before. Letting Chicagoans taste your delicious food will always be the best possible advertisement for your restaurant.
